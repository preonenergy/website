Residential Projects

Electricity tariffs have been on the rise for residential power consumers almost in every state in India. We at Preonic Energy understand the challenges faced by billions of such residential power consumers PAN-India and have developed some of the most innovative and efficient solutions to cater to such needs. Our solutions cater to both individual private residences as well as moderate to large apartment complexes.

If you pay anything above ₹ 5.00 per kWh towards your electricity bills, you should definitely reach out to us by calling us or submitting an enquiry online right away

Commercial Projects

Whether you have a small retail shop or a large manufacturing facility, solar is a great option to help you meet your sustainability goals and provide a strong return on your financial investment. Preonic will evaluate your available roof and ground space, electricity consumption, and budget to tailor a system that best meets your needs

Installing solar does much more for your business than save on electricity costs. Installing solar power will also signal your companies commitment to reducing your carbon footprint and attract like-minded employees and customers.
